Beginners Course

This course is an introducton to kayaking especially designed for those who have never kayaked before. You will learn the basics of kayaking, including how to handle the equipment, basic paddling techniques, and safety on the water. This course is perfect for anyone looking to start their kayaking journey and explore the beautiful world of whitewater.


  • Basic paddling techniques
  • Equipment handling
  • Introduction to water navigation
  • Building confidence on calm waters

Duration: 1 day, 3 hours| Location: Soča river, from Čezsoča to Boka | Price: Contact for details

Intermediate Course

This course is designed for those who want to take their kayaking to the next level. Here you will learn how to navigate wild water, master more advanced wild water techniques, and improve your Eskimo roll to perfection.


  • More advanced techniques
  • Reading river currents and rapids
  • Eskimo roll techniques
  • Basic rescue skills

Advanced Course

The advanced course is for those who want to experience the extreme side of kayaking. You will be crossing Class III to IV wild water, learning rescue techniques, and improving your wild water skills.


  • High-level river running
  • Advanced rescue and safety techniques

Duration: 3 days 3 - 4 hours per session | Location: Soča river; Srpenica to Trnovo 1 or 2 | Price: Contact for details

Hello, I'm Marko Petek

Hello, my name is Marko Petek, and I come from Slovenia. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Sports Science and am a professional kayaker. I've been training in kayak slalom since the age of 10, taking my first strokes on the Soča River. I've competed at the highest level, including World and European Championships as a member of the Slovenian national team. More recently, I've fallen in love with creeking. This passion has taken me to New Zealand, Norway, Italy, Austria, and the Zambezi. I am eager to expand my list of places I've kayaked and continue chasing new whitewater adventures around the world.
